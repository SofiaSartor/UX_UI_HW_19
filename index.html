COLLAB

COLLAB is a mobile networking application inspired for creatives. Although there are many digital networking platforms, emerging creatives find it difficult to network and connect within their communities. COLLAB offers a solution to this issue by giving creators a platform to share their work, collaborate with fellow creators, and to search for inspiration.
